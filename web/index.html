How to use

  1. Allow camera and microphone access.
  2. Position your face in the webcam view.
  3. Start the monitor and follow the live status.

This POC uses browser APIs to approximate driver drowsiness and distraction. It is not a certified safety system.

Troubleshooting

  • WebAssembly errors: Use Chrome or Firefox (latest versions)
  • Permission denied: Allow camera and microphone in browser settings
  • Face not detected: Ensure good lighting and face centered in camera
  • Audio not working: Check microphone permissions and volume
